Few of the NGOs around the world and thier purpose

PEOPLE FOR ANIMALS
Country: India
Location: New Delhi, India
Founded by: Maneka Gandhi in: 1992
Purpose: maintenance of animal rights and welfare in India, with an aim to establish various animal welfare centres across India’s 600 districts.
Link: https://peopleforanimalsindia.org/index.php

New Jersey Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (NJSPCA Humane Police)
Country: United States
Location: New Jersey, United States
Founded in: January 2018
Purpose: prevent animal cruelty and investigate cases of animal cruelty in New Jersey
Link: https://jspca.org.je/

American Bird Conservancy (ABC)
Country: Western Hemisphere
Location: The Plains, Virginia, United States.
Founded in: 1994
Purpose: saving native birds and their habitats, saving birds nearing extinction
Link: https://abcbirds.org/

WildAid
Country: United States
Location: San Francisco, California, United States
Founded by: Steven R. Galster & Michael C. Mitchell in: 1995
Purpose: “The illegal wildlife trade is a multi-billion dollar global industry largely driven by consumer demand in expanding economies. While most wildlife conservation groups focus on scientific studies and anti-poaching efforts, WildAid works to reduce global consumption of wildlife products and to increase local support for conservation efforts. We also work with governments and partners to protect fragile marine reserves from illegal fishing and shark finning, to enhance public and political will for anti-poaching efforts, and to reduce climate change impacts.”
Link: https://wildaid.org/
